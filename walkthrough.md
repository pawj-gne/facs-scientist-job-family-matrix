# Site Walkthrough

This guide will help you navigate the FACS Scientist Job Family Matrix site and get the most out of its features.

## What This Site Is For

This site documents a **career progression framework** for Flow Cytometry Core Facility Scientists. It's designed to help you:

- Understand the three-tier promotion model (Operator ‚Üí Consultant ‚Üí Investigator)
- Frame your work as measurable impact for promotion cases
- Collaborate with colleagues on refining the framework

## The Discussion Forum

The forum lets you discuss the framework with colleagues. Here's how to use it:

### Accessing the Forum

1. Click the **Forum** button in the header (top right) or sidebar
2. You'll need a GitHub account to participate
3. First-time users: Enter your GitHub personal access token (see [Forum Setup Guide]({{ '/FORUM-GUIDE' | relative_url }}))

### Starting a Discussion

1. Go to the Forum page
2. Click **New Discussion**
3. Choose a category (Feedback, Question, or Suggestion)
4. Write your post and submit

### Discussing Specific Text

You can highlight any text on the site to start a discussion about it:

1. Select text on any page (10-1000 characters)
2. A **"Discuss this"** button appears above your selection
3. Click it to open the forum with your quote pre-filled

### Notification Badge

The forum badge (in the header) shows unread activity:
- Red number = new posts or replies since your last visit
- Click to see what's new

## Tips for Getting the Most Out of This Site

### For Understanding the Framework

- Read the **Three-Tier Model** first to understand the philosophy
- Use the **Promotion Criteria** as a quick reference
- Check **Worked Examples** to see how tiers apply to real scenarios

### For Writing Promotion Cases

- Start with **Reframing Strategies** to learn how to frame your work
- Use **Worked Examples** as templates for your own contributions
- Focus on **impact and outcomes**, not just duties

### For Giving Feedback

- Use the **Forum** for general discussions
- Use **text highlighting** to comment on specific passages
- Be constructive ‚Äî this framework is a work in progress

## Quick Reference: Tier Language

When writing about your work, use tier-appropriate verbs:

| Tier | Use These Verbs |
|:-----|:----------------|
| **Sci 2** (Operator) | Executes, Maintains, Performs, Operates |
| **Sci 3** (Consultant) | Consults, Designs, Troubleshoots, Advises |
| **Sci 4** (Investigator) | Leads, Validates, Publishes, Innovates |
