# Immunoplex Lab: Promotion Project Examples

**Purpose:** Concrete examples of promotable work at each Scientist tier for the Immunoplex (Luminex/bead-based multiplex) platform.

---

## Tier Overview: Role-Scope Progression

| Tier | Persona | Focus | Immunoplex Scope |
|:-----|:--------|:------|:-----------------|
| **Sci 2 (SE4/5)** | Operator | Run Samples | Executes standard Luminex assays; performs routine QC; basic troubleshooting |
| **Sci 3 (SE6)** | Consultant | Troubleshoot & Optimize | Customizes panels; advises on assay selection; resolves matrix effects |
| **Sci 4 (SE7)** | Investigator | Method Development & Publication | Develops novel assays; validates new platforms; publishes methodology |

## Scientific Contribution & Technical Expertise

### Experimental Design & Analysis

| Tier | Experience Summary | Example Activities |
|:-----|:-------------------|:-------------------|
| **Sci 2** | Follows established protocols for standard panels | Executed 10-plex cytokine assays per SOP; prepared samples using validated dilution schemes; ran xPONENT software and exported standard curve data |
| **Sci 3** | Advises researchers and optimizes assay performance | Consulted with PIs on panel selection for specific biological questions; optimized sample dilutions to reduce matrix interference; troubleshot high background and recommended corrective actions |
| **Sci 4** | Designs novel assays and validates new approaches | Developed custom 40-plex biomarker panel; validated cross-reactivity across species; correlated multiplex results with orthogonal methods (ELISA, MSD) |

### Specialized Technologies

| Tier | Experience Summary | Example Activities |
|:-----|:-------------------|:-------------------|
| **Sci 2** | Operates core platform with proficiency | Operated Luminex 200 for routine sample processing; maintained bead-handling protocols; performed daily calibration and verification |
| **Sci 3** | Expert in platform capabilities and limitations | Implemented custom bead regions for novel targets; optimized coupling chemistry for in-house antibodies; trained team on advanced acquisition settings |
| **Sci 4** | Evaluates and onboards new technologies | Led FLEXMAP 3D platform evaluation and implementation; validated high-throughput automation integration; published platform comparison methodology |

### Knowledge Application

| Tier | Experience Summary | Example Activities |
|:-----|:-------------------|:-------------------|
| **Sci 2** | Applies established knowledge correctly | Applied manufacturer protocols accurately; recognized common failure modes (bead aggregation, high CVs); escalated complex issues appropriately |
| **Sci 3** | Applies deep technical knowledge to solve problems | Diagnosed matrix interference patterns across sample types; implemented custom standard curve fits for low-abundance analytes; developed troubleshooting decision trees |
| **Sci 4** | Generates new knowledge for the field | Identified novel sources of assay variability; developed QC acceptance criteria adopted facility-wide; contributed to manufacturer protocol improvements |

### Dissemination of Findings

| Tier | Experience Summary | Example Activities |
|:-----|:-------------------|:-------------------|

| **Sci 4** | Publishes methodology and presents externally | First-author publication on multiplex optimization; presented at CYTO/AAI conferences; led webinars on platform best practices |
